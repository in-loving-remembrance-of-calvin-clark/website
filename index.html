God’s love lasts forever

1 Let not your heart be troubled. You believe in God, believe also in me. 2 In my Father's house there are many mansions. If not, I would have told you: because I go to prepare a place for you. 3 And if I shall go, and prepare a place for you, I will come again, and will take you to myself; that where I am, you also may be. 4 And whither I go you know, and the way you know. — John 14:1–4 Douay-Rheims Bible

A Memory of Calvin Clark

Calvin's kindness, quiet strength, and joyful spirit was shown to me. May these memories be a gentle comfort and a steady light for all who loved him.
